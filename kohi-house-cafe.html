The origin

Kōhī
2019Founded

Born from a love letter to slow mornings.

What began as a small corner in Mumbai's Andheri neighbourhood has grown into a destination — a place where the ritual of coffee becomes an act of presence. We believe the best cup you'll ever have is the one you weren't rushing through.

Our beans are sourced directly from family farms in Coorg, Araku Valley, and Chikmagalur — relationships built over years, not transactions. Every roast is a conversation between origin and craft.
